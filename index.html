Defensibility

Data compounds.
Moat deepens.

📊
Data Moat
Structured behavioural reference data across roles, industries, and company types. Benchmarks competitors cannot buy — only earned check by check.
🔗
Workflow Moat
Once embedded in ATS workflows (Greenhouse, Lever), removing Vouch requires both technical migration and change management. Switching cost compounds.
🛡️
Trust Graph
A growing graph of candidate-reference credibility. Models trained on who actually performs vs. who only looks good on paper. Network effect compounds with every check.
⚖️
Regulatory Moat (PIPEDA)
PIPEDA-native from day one. Canadian data residency (AWS ca-central-1), explicit referee consent, right-to-delete. U.S. competitors would need non-trivial engineering investment to match.
